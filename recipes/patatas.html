Patatas Bravas Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Toss the potato cubes with olive oil, salt, paprika, and cayenne pepper.
  3. Spread the potatoes on a baking sheet and roast for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
  4. In a small bowl, mix mayonnaise with tomato sauce to create the dipping sauce.
  5. Serve the roasted potatoes hot with the dipping sauce on the side.
